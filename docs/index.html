This is the typical user journey a spaza shop owner go through on daily bases

Before mam ntombela opens the shop, she makes sure that the shop is clean and tidy before business begins. One important part that she never misses is ordering the right quantity of stock to sell and stock in general. She thinks of all the plans in her head and writes them down on paper like the number of products needed, supplier prices and general costs like electricity, equipment, etc. From there she opens her shop and customers start coming in to buy whatever they need. Mam ntombela does everything by her head when running the shop like remembering prices, giving change, etc. She comes across challenges like customers not paying full amounts and letting them off the hook, plus stock running out unexpectedly during business hours, which affects both her business and customers. Also, she does not become aware of products that have expired because she has got a lot in her mind, which can be unfair for the customer from a accountability point of view. Another challenge she encounters is a lack of records like basic financial reports on her business, which minimize chances of her getting money from financial institutions.

The App that we developed is called "Zonke", which is a Zulu word meaning All, The free version of the app contains six options on the main menu which are :

Stock Management- it Allows use to add the stock you bought to the system.
Combo generator - Which is the special feature of our product, it use a feature which use a smart algorithms to check through the stock in the shop make a combo based on items selling rate, expiring date and profit and item make.
Profit gain and loss Calculator- the feature atomaticaly calculates the profit each items made so far and losess.
Sales - Show the amount of stock sold
Memory - Where the user can acess the information saved eg. history of stock bought
Updates - The feature allows the user to check for new update and installs
